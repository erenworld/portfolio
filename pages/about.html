About Eren

Few words to myself

More than anything, I want to make lots of stuff. I want to make articles, books, websites, music, companies, systems, apps, and especially new ideas. This shapes most of my life decisions. Saying no to almost everything, so I can have lots of time for making.

I also want to learn lots of stuff, especially different approaches to thinking and living. That's why I read so much non-fiction, and want to keep moving around the world. I connect with those who stretch, strive, and grow. I can't relate to those who chill, hang out, watch TV, party, etc.

I've optimized my life for creating and learning. I've cut out most things from my life that most normal people do — (like hanging out or media consumption) — in pursuit of my bigger goal.
