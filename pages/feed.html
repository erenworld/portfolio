Writing • Tuesday - February 20, 2024

cosmosa.com - Waitlist launched for early access

Campcalm, an upcoming project Nils (@nilseller) and I (@flornkm) are working on, is now open for early access sign-ups. We're building a platform for designers and developers to find and share fonts. Not only a Google Fonts alternative, but a tool that makes it a lot easier to find and try out new fonts.

We have a lot of ideas which we are currently evaluating and we're excited to share more about it in the upcoming weeks. If you're interested in early access, sign up on missingfont.com and we'll notify you when it's ready for you.
