Making better decisions is one of the best skills we can develop.

Eren Turkoglu

You always have a choice to change things. Take a moment to appreciate where you are now. Everything happens for a reason. Don't compare yourself. It's so easy to fall into the trap of believing that everyone you're friends with on Facebook or following on Instagram has their shit together and is years ahead of you at whatever goal it is you want to achieve.

The trouble is, when we do this, we are comparing ourselves to a semi-reality. Our social media accounts are just the highlights, not the full picture. Every skill you acquire doubles your odds of success. Defend your time. I really value my time on this earth. I read a lot. I meditate. Escape competition through authenticity.

Optimism, obsession, self belief and clear communication are how things get started. Compound yourself. Your relationships and your skills. Learn to think independently. Get good at sales. Make it easy to take risks. Work hard on the right thing. Extreme people get extreme results. Take care of you. - You are what you think. What do you think 80% of the time ? And above all, have fun.

Be satisfied. Not a maximiser. Learn from the mistakes of others. Every skill you acquire doubles your odds of success. You can code ? Learn design. You can sell ? Learn online businesses You can speak publicly ? Learn to create an offer The best entrepreneurs are full-stacks. They have a tons of skills. Sales, paid ads, persuasion, coding or speaking. The more skills you have, the more valuable you become. Be curious and learn from the best. Observe them.

Don't reinvent the wheel. Everything is here. Steal like an artist from others. Focus If you want to become successful in business, you should learn to focus. Focus is crucial. If you can't sit and learn something for 2 3 hours, you will broke for the rest of your life. How to focus like a monk ? Cut all the distractions from your life. Bad food, toxic people, noisy environment. Join people that are capable of focusing. Eat well, exercise daily and sleep minimum 7 hours.
