Discipline

Discipline is the strongest form of self-love.

Eren Turkoglu

Nov 01, 2023 Â· 102 views

Discipline is the strongest form of self-love. It means ignoring today's desires for tomorrow's needs. The "you" of the future depends on the "you" of today; it reveals the commitment you have to your dreams and the promises you have made to yourself. It distinguishes between those who talk the talk and those who walk the walk.

I've learned one thing from Jeff Bezos. It's the framework he used to make a risky decision. He had the choice of continuing in a dream job on Wall Street and living a comfortable life, but he also wanted to put his idea into action. Create the world's largest online bookstore. Both paths required energy. To make his choice, he projected himself into the future.

As Jeff Bezos at 80, he looked back and asked himself "Am I going to regret this?". The answer was yes. He warned his superior and then resigned for his dream. The real question is: Will you regret not doing it?
