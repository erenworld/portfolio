Happiness

You can achieve happiness without financial wealth. Most of us recognize you're not going to buy your way to happiness.

Eren Turkoglu

Nov 01, 2023 · 102 views

It’s difficult to write. I practice it everyday. I don’t know what to write but I write. Practicing force me to think. My brain is working. All the humans can write. You don’t money or funds. A paper and you can push your thoughts to the next level. The fundamental thing that I learned from writing is the clarity that gives me. When you see the most talented people in the world. Their mind are clear.

The trouble is, when we do this, we are comparing ourselves to a semi-reality. Our social media accounts are just the highlights, not the full picture. Every skill you acquire doubles your odds of success. Defend your time. I really value my time on this earth. I read a lot. I meditate. Escape competition through authenticity.

Optimism, obsession, self belief and clear communication are how things get started. Compound yourself. Your relationships and your skills. Learn to think independently. Get good at sales. Make it easy to take risks. Work hard on the right thing. Extreme people get extreme results. Take care of you. - You are what you think. What do you think 80% of the time ? And above all, have fun.
